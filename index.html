About Me

Hi! I’m a front-end web developer based out of Toronto, Canada. I have an educational background in Electronic Engineering from Seneca College. My diploma gave me a strong foundation to change directions and become a self-taught web developer.

COVID-19 shook up my world — for good and bad. I was laid off and had a lot of time on my hands. I knew I wanted more for myself and would spend every day for the next 3 months learning code. Coding did not click with me in any of my programming classes. It wasn’t until I found Team Treehouse that I fell in love with coding and front-end web development.
