About

Hi! I’m a front-end web developer based in Toronto, Canada. In 2019, I graduated from Seneca College with a diploma in Electronic Engineering. My diploma gave me a strong foundation and allowed me the opportunity to change directions and become a self-taught web developer.

COVID-19 shook up my world — for good and bad. I was laid off and had a lot of time on my hands. I knew I wanted more for myself and decided I would spend every day for the next 3 months learning code.

Before finding Team Treehouse, I didn’t think coding was a good fit for me. Once I was introduced to Team Treehouse and started up with the projects I quickly fell in love with coding and front-end web development.
